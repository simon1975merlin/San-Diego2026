Family Travel Tips

Make the most of your San Diego adventure 💡

☀️
Sunscreen is Non-Negotiable
San Diego sun is strong even on cloudy days. Pack SPF 50+, reapply every 2 hours, and get rash guards for the kids at the beach.
🎟️
Buy Tickets in Advance
Pre-purchase Zoo, SeaWorld, and LEGOLAND online to save 20–30%. Consider CityPASS for major savings across multiple attractions!
🚗
Parking Tips
Balboa Park and Old Town have free lots. For downtown use ParkWhiz or SpotHero apps to prepay for cheaper spots.
🌊
Beach Safety
Always swim at lifeguard beaches. Mission Beach and Pacific Beach have year-round guards. If caught in a rip current, swim parallel to shore.
🧴
Morning Fog is Normal!
The marine layer burns off by noon leaving a gorgeous sunny afternoon — don't panic if it's cloudy in the morning!
📱
Add This App to Home Screen
iPhone: Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: Chrome → Menu → Add to Home Screen. Works offline too!
🌡️
Perfect Weather Window
July 16–23: highs 74–79°F, lows in the mid-60s, zero rain. You picked the ideal week — essentially perfect weather!
🍜
Taco Tuesday is Real
San Diego is the taco capital of California. Find a local taqueria for $1–3 tacos. Locals' fave: carne asada or fish tacos.
